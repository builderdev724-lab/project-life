# 🇪🇪 Estonia: The Digital Republic

Imagine a country where opening a business takes minutes, taxes are filed online, medical records are securely accessible, and citizens can vote from anywhere in the world[cite: 1]. While this may sound like a vision of the future, it is already a reality in **Estonia**[cite: 1].

Despite being one of Europe's smallest countries, Estonia has become a global benchmark for digital governance by integrating technology into almost every aspect of public administration[cite: 1]. Through secure digital identity, interconnected government services, and a digital-first mindset, Estonia has transformed how citizens interact with their government[cite: 1].
