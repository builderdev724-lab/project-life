# 🔄 Weekly Flow

This defines how we move through the whole month.

## How it works

- Every **Sunday**, we pick a new country to explore for the month.
- **Week 1** — Discuss the **Country** (overview, general vibe, why it caught our interest).
- **Week 2** — Discuss the **Culture** of the country.
- **Week 3** — Discuss the **Language(s)** of the country.
- **Week 4** — Discuss the famous, beautiful **Places**.
- **Week 5+** — If the month has a 5th week, we keep going on the same country's leftover topics, or start prepping the next country. Then the cycle begins again.
